Here are some rules for the game of rock, paper, scissors:

Hands Signals

  • Rock: A closed fist
  • Paper: A flat hand with the fingers and thumb extended, and the palm facing down
  • Scissors: A fist with the index and middle fingers fully extended

Winning combinations

Rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper, and paper beats rock

Ties

If both players choose the same hand signal, it's a tie and play continues until there's a winner
